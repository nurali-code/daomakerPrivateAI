What is Unio Coin (UNIO)?

Unio Coin (UNIO) is the core token of the Unioverse, allowing users to create, own, and monetize content across an expansive gaming ecosystem. UNIO fuels every aspect of the platform—from minting user-generated content to trading in-game assets across different titles. UNIO is also designed to provide status within Unioverse. Staking UNIO provides access to rare assets, features and perks within the Unioverse ecosystem.

What makes Unio Coin (UNIO) unique?

Unio Coin (UNIO) isn’t just another token; it’s the creative engine behind Unioverse, a groundbreaking sci-fi franchise and game development platform. Here’s what makes Unioverse stand out from other game platforms:

  • User-Generated Content (UGC): With UNIO, players have the power to create, customize, and mint one-of-a-kind items that are permanently stored on the blockchain. From weapons and hoverbikes to heroes, starship, star bases, and more these assets can be used across multiple Unioverse games.
  • Interoperability: Unioverse flips the script by prioritizing UGC. Developers build games around the items users create, ensuring that these assets are interoperable across a wide range of games within the Unioverse ecosystem. Interoperability of items increases their value and since each item exists on the blockchain, their achievements and progress across different games are tracked, adding even more depth and history to every item.
  • Multi-platform ecosystem: Unioverse games can exist on various platforms, including consoles, Steam (PC), and mobile appstores. All these games are tied together by the Unioverse franchise and the unique items generated by the community.
