What is Octavia (VIA)?

Octavia is at the forefront of AI technology in the Web3 space, spearheading the development of next-generation AI Assistants. This includes the Octavia Assistant, a tool revolutionising and simplifying the Web3 user experience, and the Octavia Community Manager, designed to innovatively manage and moderate Web3 communities.

Key Products:

  • Octavia Assistant: A versatile Web3 assistant proficient in assisting with research, trading, and automated trading. It's accessible across various platforms, including a dedicated app, Discord, and Telegram.
  • Octavia for Groups: A groundbreaking tool for moderating chat messages (including videos, audio, text, and images) in online communities, equipped to respond to support queries with context-aware intelligence & effective FUD removal.

Octavia for Groups Details:

Octavia for Groups is a transformative AI Community Manager, designed for moderation and community management within Telegram and Discord communities. Using Octavia's advanced technology, it accurately addresses in-context questions and maintains a friendly and safe environment by effectively enforcing bespoke community rules.

Features:

  • Moderation: Octavia autonomously moderates all text messages and is gearing up to include voice, image, and video messages, ensuring communities are free from abuse, shilling, FUD, and more.
  • Assistant: Backed by the Octavia Assistant and an integrated knowledge base, Octavia reliably provides accurate responses to project-specific queries.
  • Fully Configurable: With Octavia, communities have the freedom to adjust moderation settings, striking the perfect balance for both casual and professional environments.
  • Analytics for Web3 Communities: Octavia offers insightful analytics, tracking support requests, identifying key disruptors, and assessing conversation trends, for a comprehensive and quick overview of community health.

Octavia for Groups is rolling out to partner communities actively, steadily growing its user base and extending Octavia’s market reach.

Octavia Assistant Details:

The Octavia Assistant, already a go-to for Crypto Research on Telegram and Discord, is set to expand its offerings with trading and automation capabilities in the near future, enhancing its user-friendly and responsive support for all crypto-related needs

Features of Octavia Assistant:

Research with Octavia - Simply ask Octavia any question about a crypto project, and it will efficiently conduct the research to provide a precise answer.

Trading with Octavia - Soon, Octavia will have the capability to execute trades on your behalf, so you could, for example, ask Octavia to buy Bitcoin for you directly from your phone.

Automation with Octavia - Octavia is also set to offer trade automation, allowing you to set commands like “Buy $500 in BTC when the Bitcoin ETF is approved.”

Real-World Applications:

Octavia's tools are being actively rolled out to partner communities, demonstrating their effectiveness in real-world settings. From managing community interactions to facilitating informed trading decisions, Octavia's AI solutions are proving indispensable in the contemporary digital world.

Octavia (VIA) revenue streams

Octavia has established a robust revenue model that not only supports our operational sustainability but also contributes to the continuous growth and development of our services. Our revenue streams are primarily derived from the strategic use of our native token, $VIA, and various service charges. Here's how we generate revenue:

  • Premium Feature Access with $VIA: Users can access premium features, such as automated trading (e.g., executing a buy order when a specific event occurs), by using $VIA.
  • Feature Voting Using $VIA: $VIA holders have the privilege of voting on upcoming features and determining the priority of their development, fostering a community-driven approach to our platform's evolution.
  • Commission on AI-PoweredSwaps and Orders: We charge a commission in $VIA for transactions made through our AI, including swaps and orders, contributing to our revenue stream.
  • Revenue from Moderation Services: We generate income from moderation services offered to communities, accepting payments in $VIA or stablecoins like USDT/USDC. This revenue is fully reinvested into the token economy in the first year, and 35% thereafter once we reach break-even. The burn rate for this is $35k per month.

Through these diverse revenue channels, Octavia ensures a steady flow of income, crucial for sustaining and scaling our operations in the dynamic Web3 and crypto markets.
