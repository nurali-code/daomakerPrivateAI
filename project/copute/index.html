What is Copute (COPU)?

Copute.ai redefines GPU power as a community-owned resource, where node operators, AI creators, and game devs all share in the rewards—making Web3 tech practical and useful for AI and gaming. Our ecosystem goal is to democratize high-performance GPU computing

We offer a decentralized computing network through our unique "Cloud + Chain" DePIN Web3 Protocol, making GPU power more accessible and cost-effective. Our solutions support developers and users in Cloud Gaming and AI, enhancing the way GPU computing power is utilized and enjoyed globally.

Copute.ai envisions a Web3 Aggregator Ecosystem where efforts such as node operation, AI contributions, and gaming content creation are incentivized through our Mine-Share-As-A-Service model with the following solutions and services:

Cloud Gaming Solution Suite:

  • Virtualization Infrastructure: Low-latency cloud gaming with P2P PC farming
  • GPU Rendering Pass-through: Exceptional graphics and rendering capabilities
  • Network Transmission: Smooth and immersive cloud gaming experiences

AI Cloud Computing Platform:

  • Decentralized Computing Engine: Unmatched GPU rendering scalability
  • P2P Network Structure: Enhanced rendering efficiency through seamless resource sharing
  • Shared System Resources: Optimized utilization and cost-effectiveness

Blockchain Technology and Community Engagement: 

  • Modular Approach: Scalability and flexibility as the network grows
  • Decentralization: Secure, transparent resource allocation

Ecosystem: Through the on-chain management and token use cases, we can drive community engagement and ecosystem adoption.

What is Copute (COPU) roadmap?

Copute (COPU) revenue streams

1. Cloud Gaming and AI Services:

Our main revenue stream comes from providing cloud gaming and AI solutions, especially for businesses like esports cafes, hotels, and gaming companies. These companies need GPU power, and we offer it through our GPU marketplace, where they can rent the cards they need. This setup gives us a steady flow of income while supporting industries that rely on high-performance computing.

2. Token Utility:

Our token is at the heart of everything we do. It’s not just a means of exchange; it powers our entire ecosystem. We generate revenue through:

  • Staking: Users can stake their tokens, and while they earn rewards, we also get a small cut to support network security and operations.
  • Node Rewards: By encouraging community members to run validator nodes, we create a decentralized and secure network. In return, we earn transaction fees from the activity on the network.
  • Payments and Discounts: Our token is used to pay for services within our ecosystem, whether it’s for cloud gaming or AI solutions. Offering discounts for token payments keeps the token in circulation and creates a consistent revenue stream.

3. Partnerships and Business Development:

We’ve built strong partnerships, particularly in the APAC region, which help us scale up. Through collaborations with gaming and AI industry leaders, we bring in revenue through shared ventures, licensing deals, and mutual business development efforts. This not only expands our reach but also strengthens our ecosystem.

4. Community Engagement:

Our community is crucial to our growth. We make sure they are involved in every step, rewarding them for completing community tasks, staking, and providing feedback on our services. These community-driven activities help grow the ecosystem organically, creating a virtuous cycle where more engagement leads to more revenue.
